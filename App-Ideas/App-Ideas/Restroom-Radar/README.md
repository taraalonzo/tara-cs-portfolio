# Restroom Radar

## Concept

Restroom Radar is an app designed to help users find public restrooms near their current location and quickly understand whether those restrooms are accessible, safe, clean, and practical to use.

Although the app is especially useful for women, families, disabled users, travelers, and people in unfamiliar areas, it can be used by anyone who needs reliable restroom information.

## Core Features

* Search for nearby public restrooms
* View restroom type and accessibility information
* See whether the restroom is free to use or requires a purchase
* Check gender availability:

  * Women’s restroom
  * Men’s restroom
  * Gender-neutral restroom
  * Family restroom
* Identify restroom layout:

  * Single-user restroom
  * Multiple stalls
  * Family room
* View accessibility details:

  * Handicap accessible
  * Baby changing station
  * Space for groups or caregivers
* View restroom supplies:

  * Paper towels
  * Hand dryers
  * Soap
  * Toilet paper
* Rate restroom quality from 0–5:

  * Cleanliness
  * Stocked supplies
  * Safety
  * Privacy
  * Overall comfort
* Add notes such as:

  * “Two-girl bathroom trip”
  * “Whole group can come”
  * “Avoid at night”
  * “Purchase required”
  * “Very clean”
  * “Single stall only”

## Possible Users

* Women traveling alone or in groups
* Parents with children
* Disabled users
* People with medical needs
* Delivery drivers
* Road trip travelers
* Students
* Event attendees
* Anyone in an unfamiliar area

## Goal

The goal of Restroom Radar is to make finding a usable public restroom faster, safer, and less stressful by providing real-world information from actual users.
